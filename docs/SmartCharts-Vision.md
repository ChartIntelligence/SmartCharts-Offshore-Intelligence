# Smart Charts

## Offshore Intelligence Platform

## Mission

Smart Charts combines oceanographic data, satellite intelligence, offshore structures, weather information, and artificial intelligence to help offshore captains make better decisions.

## Vision

Create the world's leading explainable offshore intelligence platform.

Smart Charts does not replace the captain. It organizes complex ocean information into clear, actionable intelligence.
